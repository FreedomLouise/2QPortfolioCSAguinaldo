What is Bleach Brave Souls?

Bleach Brave Souls is a mobile, PC, and PlayStation game based on the Bleach anime. Developed and published by KLab Global Pte. Ltd. in 2015, it recently celebrated its 10th anniversary in September. The game features the complete Bleach storyline, including the latest anime adaptation (with updates to come as Cour 4 is released), with the main story covering the main arcs and the filler episodes available in a separate side story section. Players can access all characters from Bleach, both canonical and filler. Additionally, the game includes characters from Burn the Witch, another story by Bleach creator Tite Kubo, which explores the United Kingdom (west) side of Soul Society 10 years after the final arc of Bleach (Soul Society is the name of the place in the afterlife).
